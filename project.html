Book Your Safari

Experience the world-famous wildebeest migration and incredible wildlife viewing in Kenya's most popular reserve.

The Masai Mara National Reserve is Kenya's most famous wildlife conservation area. Known for the annual wildebeest migration (July to October), it offers year-round wildlife viewing with abundant lions, elephants, buffalos, leopards, and rhinos.

Our packages include:

  • Professional safari guide
  • Comfortable 4x4 vehicle with roof hatch
  • Accommodation in handpicked lodges or camps
  • All meals and bottled water
  • Park entrance fees
  • Airport transfers
